# Customer Segments - StyleFit Athletic

## High-Value Segments

### VIP Gold Members
**Size:** 156 customers  
**Criteria:**
- Loyalty tier: Gold (1000+ points)
- Lifetime value: $1,500+
- Average order value: $180
- Purchase frequency: 4+ times/year

**Behavior:**
- Highly engaged with emails (60%+ open rate)
- Leave reviews frequently
- Refer friends
- Shop new arrivals first

**Campaign Strategy:**
- Early access to sales
- Exclusive product previews
- VIP-only discounts (20-30%)
- Personal shopping assistance offers

---

### Inactive Premium Customers
**Size:** 847 customers  
**Criteria:**
- Last purchase: 45-90 days ago
- Previous tier: Silver or Gold
- Lifetime value: $500+
- Historical engagement: High

**Behavior:**
- Used to shop regularly (monthly)
- Stopped engaging with emails
- Previously left positive reviews
- High cart abandonment rate recently

**Campaign Strategy:**
- Win-back offers (25-30% off)
- "We miss you" personalized messaging
- Highlight new products they'd love
- Survey: "What can we improve?"

**Predicted Re-engagement:**
- 28.5% likely to return with offer
- Average return order: $120
- ROI: 7.3x

---

### New Customers (First 30 Days)
**Size:** 234 customers  
**Criteria:**
- First purchase within last 30 days
- Haven't made second purchase yet
- Engaged with welcome series

**Behavior:**
- High email open rates (45%+)
- Browse website frequently
- Add to cart but don't convert
- Interested in loyalty program

**Campaign Strategy:**
- Second purchase incentive (15% off)
- Educational content (how to care for products)
- Loyalty program benefits highlight
- Size/fit guide resources

---

### Cart Abandoners
**Size:** 423 customers  
**Criteria:**
- Added items to cart in last 7 days
- Didn't complete purchase
- Average cart value: $145

**Behavior:**
- Considering specific products
- May be price-sensitive
- Often return after reminder

**Campaign Strategy:**
- Cart reminder emails (3-email series)
- Limited-time discount (10%)
- Free shipping offer
- Product reviews from other customers

---

### Seasonal Shoppers
**Size:** 312 customers  
**Criteria:**
- Purchase 1-2x per year
- Always during sales periods
- Price-conscious

**Behavior:**
- Wait for promotions
- Stock up during sales
- Don't engage between purchases

**Campaign Strategy:**
- Major sale notifications
- "Early bird" sale access
- Bundle deals
- Seasonal previews

---

### Gift Buyers
**Size:** 189 customers  
**Criteria:**
- Purchase patterns suggest gifting
- Shop around holidays/birthdays
- Often buy gift cards
- Higher cart diversity

**Behavior:**
- One-time or infrequent shoppers
- Need gift guides/recommendations
- Appreciate gift wrapping options

**Campaign Strategy:**
- Holiday gift guides
- Gift card promotions
- "Perfect for..." collections
- Gift wrapping highlights

---

### Brand Advocates
**Size:** 94 customers  
**Criteria:**
- Left 5+ product reviews
- Shared on social media
- Referred 3+ friends
- High engagement rate

**Behavior:**
- Extremely loyal
- Promote brand organically
- Provide valuable feedback
- Respond to surveys

**Campaign Strategy:**
- Exclusive ambassador program
- Early access to everything
- Request feedback on new products
- Special recognition/rewards

---

### At-Risk Churn
**Size:** 567 customers  
**Criteria:**
- Last purchase: 90-180 days ago
- Declining email engagement
- Tier: Bronze or no tier
- Low lifetime value (<$200)

**Behavior:**
- Opened fewer emails over time
- No website visits in 60+ days
- Didn't respond to previous win-back

**Campaign Strategy:**
- Last-chance win-back (40% off)
- Feedback survey with incentive
- Highlight what's new since they left
- Low-commitment offers (free gift with purchase)

## Recommended Campaign Frequency

**VIP Gold:** Weekly  
**Inactive Premium:** 1x for win-back, then monthly if re-engaged  
**New Customers:** Daily (welcome series), then weekly  
**Cart Abandoners:** 3 emails over 5 days, then stop  
**Seasonal Shoppers:** Only during major sales (4-6x/year)  
**Gift Buyers:** Heavy around holidays, light rest of year  
**Brand Advocates:** 2-3x per week  
**At-Risk Churn:** Final win-back attempt, then suppress
