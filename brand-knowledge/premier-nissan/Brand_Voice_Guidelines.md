## Brand Voice Attributes

### 1. Professional Yet Friendly
We're experts, but we don't talk down to customers. We explain things clearly without jargon.

**Do:** "Let's take a look at what your car needs to stay running smoothly."
**Don't:** "Your vehicle requires immediate attention to the CV joint assembly."

### 2. Trustworthy & Transparent
We build long-term relationships through honesty. No hidden fees, no surprise charges.

**Do:** "Here's exactly what we found and what it'll cost‚Äîno surprises."
**Don't:** "There may be additional charges depending on what we find."

### 3. Knowledgeable & Helpful
We share our expertise to help customers make informed decisions.

**Do:** "Most cars need an oil change every 5,000 miles, but here's what we recommend for your specific model..."
**Don't:** "You need an oil change. Do you want to schedule it?"

### 4. Community-Focused
We're part of this community. We sponsor local teams, support local causes, and know many customers by name.

**Do:** "We've been your neighbors for 25 years..."
**Don't:** "As a leading regional automotive dealer..."

### 5. Value-Conscious (Not Cheap)
We're not the cheapest, and we don't pretend to be. We offer fair prices for quality service.

**Do:** "Quality service at a fair price‚Äîthat's the Premier promise."
**Don't:** "Lowest prices guaranteed!"

## Tone by Channel

### Email Marketing
- **Tone:** Warm, helpful, personal
- **Length:** Keep under 150 words (proven higher engagement)
- **Personalization:** Always use first name + vehicle when available
- **CTA:** Clear, single action

**Example:**
```
Hi [First Name],

Your [Vehicle] has been a great companion for [X] miles! 
Around this time, most Altimas benefit from a quick check-up 
to keep running smoothly.

that includes oil change, tire rotation, and our 21-point inspection.

Book your appointment and we'll have you back on the road in no time.

[Book Service Now]

See you soon,
The Premier Nissan Team
```

### SMS
- **Tone:** Brief, friendly, urgent when appropriate
- **Length:** Under 160 characters
- **Use for:** Appointment reminders, time-sensitive offers only

**Example:**
```
Hi [Name]! Your service appointment is tomorrow at 10 AM. 
Reply YES to confirm or call us to reschedule. - Premier Nissan
```

### Social Media
- **Tone:** Casual, community-focused, visual
- **Facebook:** Customer stories, community involvement, longer posts OK
- **Instagram:** Behind-the-scenes, visual content, short captions
- **LinkedIn:** Employee spotlights, company news, professional

## Messaging by Audience

### New Customers
- Focus: Build trust, remove anxiety
- Tone: Welcoming, no-pressure
- Message: "We're here to help, not push"

### Existing Customers
- Focus: Appreciation, relationship
- Tone: Familiar, grateful
- Message: "Thanks for being part of our family"

### Lapsed Customers
- Focus: We miss you, we've improved
- Tone: Humble, inviting
- Message: "We'd love to see you again"

### VIP/Long-term Customers
- Focus: Exclusive, appreciated
- Tone: Warm, personal
- Message: "You're special to us"

## Visual Brand Elements

### Colors
- **Primary Blue:** #1B3A6D (Trust, professionalism)
- **Accent Red:** #C41E3A (Nissan heritage, energy)
- **White:** #FFFFFF (Clean, open)
- **Gray:** #4A4A4A (Body text)

### Logo Usage
- Always use approved logo files
- Minimum clear space: Height of "P" around all sides
- Never stretch, rotate, or recolor

### Photography Style
- Real customers and employees (not stock when possible)
- Bright, well-lit
- Showing genuine interactions
- Include diverse representation

## What We NEVER Do

1. **Pressure tactics** - "Buy now or miss out forever!"
2. **Fear-based messaging** - "Your car could break down any day!"
3. **Misleading claims** - "Best prices guaranteed" (unless legally verified)
4. **Negative competitor mentions** - "Unlike [competitor], we..."
5. **ALL CAPS for emphasis** - We don't yell at customers
6. **Excessive exclamation marks** - One is enough!!!
7. **Bait and switch** - Every offer must be honored as stated
8. **Generic stock photos** - Our community deserves to see real people
