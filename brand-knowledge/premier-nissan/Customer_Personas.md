# Premier Nissan - Customer Personas

## Version 2.0 | Updated: September 2025

---

## Overview

Based on analysis of 12,400 customers in our database, we've identified 5 primary personas that guide our marketing, sales, and service strategies.

---

## Persona 1: "Reliable Rachel"

### The Busy Professional

**Demographics:**
- **Age:** 35-48
- **Income:** $75,000 - $120,000
- **Occupation:** Professional (healthcare, finance, management)
- **Family:** Married, 1-2 children
- **Location:** Suburban, 15-20 min from dealership

**Vehicle Profile:**
- **Current:** Nissan Rogue or Murano (2-4 years old)
- **Mileage:** 12,000-15,000/year
- **Ownership:** Buys, doesn't lease
- **Trade Cycle:** Every 5-6 years

**Behaviors:**
- Time is her most valuable resource
- Researches online but prefers phone/in-person for booking
- Needs appointment flexibility (early morning or Saturday)
- Values quality over lowest price
- Reads reviews before making decisions
- Loyal once trust is established

**Pain Points:**
- Long wait times at service (critical issue)
- Unexpected costs or "surprises"
- Feeling upsold on unnecessary services
- Difficult scheduling process
- No loaner car available

**What She Values:**
- Fast, efficient service
- Clear, upfront pricing
- Text updates during service
- Complimentary loaner vehicle
- Same advisor each visit (relationship)

**Marketing Approach:**
- **Channel:** Email (primary), SMS (reminders only)
- **Timing:** Tuesday/Saturday 10 AM
- **Message:** Convenience, time-saving, reliability
- **Offer Type:** Maintenance packages, time-saving bundles

**Example Message:**
*"Hi Rachel, your Rogue is due for its 30K service. Book online in 60 seconds, drop off at 7 AM, and we'll have you on your way by lunch. Loaner included."*

**Lifetime Value:** $8,500+ (vehicle + service over 5 years)

## Persona 2: "Value-Seeking Victor"

### The Budget-Conscious Buyer

**Demographics:**
- **Age:** 28-42
- **Income:** $45,000 - $70,000
- **Occupation:** Skilled trades, entry-level professional
- **Family:** Single or young family
- **Location:** Mix of urban/suburban

**Vehicle Profile:**
- **Current:** Nissan Sentra, Versa, or Kicks
- **Mileage:** 15,000-20,000/year (commuter)
- **Ownership:** Buys used or certified pre-owned
- **Trade Cycle:** Keeps until it dies or major repair needed

**Behaviors:**
- Price-compares everything
- Uses coupons and waits for specials
- Does some maintenance himself (oil changes)
- Comes to dealer for complex repairs
- Active on social media, follows deals

**Pain Points:**
- Dealer prices feel "too high"
- Doesn't trust upselling
- Worried about being taken advantage of
- Can't afford unexpected repairs

**What He Values:**
- Transparent pricing (no hidden fees)
- Matching or beating independent shop prices
- Payment plan options
- Clear explanation of what's needed vs. nice-to-have
- Coupons and loyalty discounts

**Marketing Approach:**
- **Channel:** Email, Social media (Facebook deals)
- **Timing:** End of month, seasonal specials
- **Message:** Value, savings, no-pressure
- **Offer Type:** Percentage off, price match, bundle deals

**Example Message:**
*"Oil change + tire rotation for $59. That's less than the quick-lube place, and we actually know your Sentra inside and out."*

**Lifetime Value:** $3,200 (service over 6+ years, less frequent)

## Persona 3: "Loyal Larry"

### The Long-Term Customer

**Demographics:**
- **Age:** 50-68
- **Income:** $80,000 - $150,000
- **Occupation:** Established career, some retired
- **Family:** Empty nester or grandparent
- **Location:** Suburban, longtime community member

**Vehicle Profile:**
- **Current:** Nissan Maxima, Armada, or Pathfinder
- **Mileage:** 8,000-12,000/year
- **Ownership:** Has bought 3+ vehicles from us
- **Trade Cycle:** Every 4-5 years (buys new)

**Behaviors:**
- Refers friends and family regularly
- Doesn't price shop - trusts us
- Expects to be recognized and remembered
- Prefers phone calls to email
- Leaves reviews when asked

**Pain Points:**
- Feeling like "just another customer"
- New staff who don't know his history
- Being offered same deals as new customers
- Technology barriers (online scheduling)

**What He Values:**
- Personal recognition ("Hi Larry!")
- Exclusive VIP treatment
- First access to new models
- Relationship with service advisor
- Phone calls, not just emails

**Marketing Approach:**
- **Channel:** Phone, Email (personalized), Direct mail
- **Timing:** Personal outreach, not mass campaigns
- **Message:** Appreciation, VIP status, relationship
- **Offer Type:** Exclusive previews, loyalty rewards, referral bonuses

**Example Message:**
*"Larry, you've been with us for 12 years and 4 vehicles. As a thank you, your next oil change is on us. Also, the 2026 Maxima just arrivedâ€”want a private preview before we announce it?"*

**Lifetime Value:** $45,000+ (multiple vehicles + service)

## Persona 4: "Safety-First Sam"

### The Family Protector

**Demographics:**
- **Age:** 32-45
- **Income:** $65,000 - $100,000
- **Occupation:** Varies
- **Family:** Parent of young children (car seats age)
- **Location:** Suburban, good school district

**Vehicle Profile:**
- **Current:** Nissan Pathfinder, Rogue, or Armada
- **Mileage:** 14,000-18,000/year (family taxi)
- **Ownership:** Buys or leases (depends on deal)
- **Trade Cycle:** Every 3-4 years (outgrows vehicle)

**Behaviors:**
- Safety ratings are #1 priority
- Researches extensively online
- Asks lots of questions
- Wants vehicle to "last" until kids are older
- Active in parent groups (word of mouth)

**Pain Points:**
- Worry about breakdowns with kids in car
- Hidden safety issues not caught
- Pushy salespeople who don't understand family needs
- Lack of family-friendly waiting area

**What He Values:**
- Comprehensive safety inspections
- Clear explanation of any concerns
- Kid-friendly waiting area
- Car seat checks (free service)
- Peace of mind messaging

**Marketing Approach:**
- **Channel:** Email, Facebook parent groups
- **Timing:** Back-to-school, family holiday seasons
- **Message:** Safety, protection, peace of mind
- **Offer Type:** Safety inspection packages, family bundles

**Example Message:**
*"Back to school means more miles. Is your Pathfinder ready? Our 21-point safety inspection checks everything from brakes to lightsâ€”because your cargo is precious. Free this month."*

**Lifetime Value:** $12,000+ (2 vehicles + consistent service)

## Persona 5: "First-Time Fiona"

### The Young Buyer

**Demographics:**
- **Age:** 22-30
- **Income:** $35,000 - $55,000
- **Occupation:** Early career professional
- **Family:** Single, no children
- **Location:** Urban or suburban apartment

**Vehicle Profile:**
- **Current:** First Nissan (Sentra, Kicks, or Versa)
- **Mileage:** 10,000-14,000/year
- **Ownership:** Financing (first car loan often)
- **Trade Cycle:** Unknown (first major purchase)

**Behaviors:**
- Entire research process is online
- Influenced by social media and reviews
- Anxious about car maintenance (doesn't know much)
- Prefers text/chat over phone calls
- Price-sensitive but will pay for convenience

**Pain Points:**
- Intimidated by dealerships
- Doesn't understand maintenance schedules
- Worried about being overcharged
- Credit concerns for financing
- No trusted advisor to ask questions

**What She Values:**
- Educational, non-judgmental communication
- Digital-first experience (online booking, text updates)
- Clear explanation of what services mean
- Affordable maintenance plans
- Welcoming, non-intimidating environment

**Marketing Approach:**
- **Channel:** SMS, Instagram, Email
- **Timing:** Evening/weekend (young professional schedule)
- **Message:** Helpful, educational, welcoming
- **Offer Type:** First-service discount, maintenance education

## Using Personas in Campaigns

### Segmentation Rules

| Persona | CRM Identifier | Key Criteria |
|---------|---------------|--------------|
| Rachel | BUSY_PRO | Age 35-48, income >$70K, SUV owner |
| Victor | VALUE_SEEK | Income <$70K, coupon user, price-sensitive tag |
| Larry | VIP_LOYAL | 3+ purchases, 5+ years customer |
| Sam | FAMILY_SAFE | Has "family" tag, SUV/minivan, parent indicator |
| Fiona | FIRST_TIME | Age <30, first purchase, <1 year customer |

### Campaign Matching

| Campaign Type | Best Personas | Avoid |
|---------------|--------------|-------|
| Service Special | Victor, Rachel | Larry (VIP treatment instead) |
| Loyalty Rewards | Larry, Rachel | Victor (wants discounts, not points) |
| Safety Campaign | Sam, Rachel | Victor (not primary driver) |
| New Model Launch | Larry, Rachel | Victor (price-sensitive) |
| Educational | Fiona | Larry (already knows) |
