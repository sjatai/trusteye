## Discount Effectiveness Analysis

| Discount Level | Conversion Rate | Margin Impact | Net Value |
|----------------|-----------------|---------------|-----------|
| 10% off | 4.2% | -8% | Positive |
| 15% off | 6.1% | -12% | Positive (Best ROI) |
| 20% off | 6.8% | -17% | Neutral |
| 25% off | 7.2% | -22% | Negative |

**Key Finding:**
Discounts above 15% show diminishing returns. Conversion increases only 2% while margin drops 8%. 

**Recommendation:** Cap regular discounts at 15%. Use 20%+ only for win-back campaigns targeting lapsed customers with high LTV.

## Q4 Recommendations

Based on Q3 learnings:

1. **Optimal Send Configuration**
   - Day: Saturday (or Tuesday as backup)
   - Time: 10:00 AM local
   - Subject: Include emoji + personalization + question format

2. **Discount Strategy**
   - Regular campaigns: 15% max
   - Win-back (lapsed): 20-25% acceptable
   - VIP: Focus on perks, not discounts

3. **Channel Mix**
   - Primary: Email (78% customer preference)
   - Secondary: SMS for time-sensitive only
   - Support: Social for brand awareness

4. **Segment Prioritization**
   - VIP customers: Loyalty perks campaign
   - New customers: Welcome series (3 emails)
   - Lapsed: Win-back with stronger offer

5. **Riverside Location**
   - Hold marketing until service issues resolved
   - Focus on review recovery
   - Consider "We're improving" messaging
