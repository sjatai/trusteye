# Premier Nissan - Marketing Strategy 2025

## Executive Summary

Premier Nissan operates 3 locations across the metro area: Downtown, Riverside, and Valley. Our 2025 marketing strategy focuses on increasing service revenue, improving customer retention, and leveraging AI-driven personalization.

---

## Key Objectives

### 1. Service Revenue Growth
- **Target:** 20% increase in service department revenue
- **Current:** $4.2M annually
- **Goal:** $5.04M by end of 2025

### 2. Customer Retention
- **Target:** Improve retention rate from 62% to 75%
- **Strategy:** Loyalty program + personalized outreach

### 3. Review Score Improvement
- **Target:** Maintain 4.5+ star average across all locations
- **Current:** 4.3 average (Riverside lagging at 4.0)

### 4. Referral Program Launch
- **Target:** 500 new customers via referrals
- **Incentive:** $50 credit for referrer, $50 off for friend

## Target Segments

### Primary: Service Customers
- **Size:** 12,400 customers in database
- **Profile:** Purchased vehicle from us or had service in last 3 years
- **Value:** Average $450/year in service revenue
- **Communication:** Email (78% preference), SMS (45%)

### Secondary: Conquest Customers
- **Size:** Targeted via digital ads
- **Profile:** Own Nissan, not our customer
- **Value:** Potential $2,500+ in first year (trade-in + service)

### Tertiary: Lease End Customers
- **Size:** 847 customers with lease ending in 2025
- **Profile:** High conversion potential
- **Value:** Average $32,000 new vehicle purchase
